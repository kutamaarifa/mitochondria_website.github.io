Project Phoenix

Project Phoenix is an exciting collaboration between Mitocondria Energy Company, the IDC & the DBSA to create a fully South African owned fuel cell system. Project Phoenix is specifically designed for the South African market. It will be manufactured locally in a state fo the art facility.

The modularity of our system

The fuel cell system was design to provide all the benefits of modularity. The stack module forms our building blocks for our single systems. These single systems are then coupled to form a full system. The systems are designed to stack together for large multi MW applications, creating a design specifically for your energy needs.
