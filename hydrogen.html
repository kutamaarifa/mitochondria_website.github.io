Hydrogen Valley

Hydrogen is the transition gas towards a zero carbon energy future

Hydrogen Vision in South Africa

As the global energy community shifts from carbon intensive energy sources to zero carbon energy sources, we at Mitochondria believe the African continent is uniquely positioned to champion this global transition. Africa is the only continent that is rich in solar radiation, wind power, mineral resources, and subsequently hydrogen.

The Hydrogen Valley ™

One of our vision is to stimulate the hydrogen economy in our continent. Hydrogen is very versatile and will play a major role in the energy balance of the future. Hydrogen will be utilized in the following sectors:

  • Energy Generation and Storage
  • Zero-carbon future of hard to abate sectors such as steel, cement, fertilizer
  • Fertilizer
  • Export Market
